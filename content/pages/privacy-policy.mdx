# TOTR Meme Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 16, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to TOTR Meme, the AI-powered meme generator platform that helps you create viral content. This Privacy Policy explains how TOTR Meme ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you create memes and use our website at totrmeme.online.

Your privacy is important to us. We're committed to protecting your personal data while providing you with the best meme creation experience.

## Information We Collect for TOTR Meme

### Meme Creation Information
- **Game Statistics**: Your daily Thwordle scores, completion times, win streaks, and puzzle attempt history
- **Game Preferences**: Theme selections (Harry Potter, LOTR, Marvel, etc.), difficulty settings, and hint usage
- **Progress Data**: Your vocabulary improvement metrics and achievement unlocks

### Account Information
- **Registration Details**: Username, email address, and password (encrypted) when you create a Thwordle account
- **Profile Information**: Display name, avatar, and game preferences you choose to share

### Technical Data
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution for optimal Thwordle gameplay
- **Usage Analytics**: Pages visited, time spent playing word puzzles, and interaction patterns
- **Performance Data**: Game loading times and error reports to improve your Thwordle experience

## How Thwordle Uses Your Information

We use your information to:

### Enhance Your Word Game Experience
- Track your daily Thwordle progress and maintain win streaks
- Provide personalized word difficulty based on your skill level
- Save your game preferences and theme selections
- Generate vocabulary insights and improvement suggestions

### Service Operations
- Deliver daily word puzzles and maintain game functionality
- Provide customer support for Thwordle-related issues
- Send important updates about new word themes and features
- Analyze gameplay patterns to improve puzzle quality

### Community Features
- Enable leaderboards and friendly competition (with your consent)
- Share achievement badges and milestone celebrations
- Compare your Thwordle performance with friends (anonymized data)

## Data Security for Word Game Players

We implement robust security measures to protect your Thwordle data:

- **Encryption**: All game data and personal information are encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Secure Authentication**: Password protection with optional two-factor authentication
- **Regular Backups**: Your game progress is securely backed up to prevent data loss
- **Limited Access**: Only authorized personnel can access user data for support purposes

## Third-Party Services Used by Thwordle

Thwordle may integrate with:

- **Analytics Services**: To understand how players interact with word puzzles (anonymized data only)
- **Cloud Storage**: To securely store your game progress and statistics
- **Authentication Providers**: For secure login options (Google, Apple, etc.)
- **Payment Processors**: For premium Thwordle features (if applicable)

## Your Thwordle Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access Your Data**: View all personal information and game statistics we store
- **Update Information**: Modify your profile, preferences, and account details
- **Delete Account**: Remove your Thwordle account and associated game data
- **Data Portability**: Export your game statistics and progress data
- **Opt-Out**: Decline optional data collection for analytics and marketing

## Children's Privacy

Thwordle is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Game data retained while your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts**: All personal data removed within 30 days of deletion request

## International Data Transfers

If you play Thwordle from outside our primary server location, your data may be transferred internationally. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable privacy laws and use appropriate safeguards.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our word game features or legal requirements. We'll notify active Thwordle players of significant changes through:

- Email notifications to registered users
- In-game announcements during your daily puzzle
- Updates posted on our website at totrmeme.online
